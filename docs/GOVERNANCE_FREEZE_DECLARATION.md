## Statement

The Cesvi governance layer is hereby declared **frozen**.

No additional governance documents are required for normal Journey production.

Future work shall focus on authoring and improving individual Journeys.

---

## Amendment Conditions

Governance documents shall only be modified if a governance-level defect is demonstrated that cannot be resolved within an individual Journey.

Governance documents shall not be modified for:

- stylistic preferences,
- individual Journey issues,
- author opinion,
- implementation convenience.

A governance amendment requires demonstrating that the defect exists at the governance layer, not at the Chapter layer.
