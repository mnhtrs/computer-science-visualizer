## Authority of This Constitution

Future Chapters shall conform to this Constitution without exception.

The authority hierarchy is, and shall remain:

**Constitution → Governance → Architecture → Curriculum → Journeys → Runtime**

---

## Amendment Conditions

This Constitution shall not be modified for:

- stylistic improvements,
- educational preferences,
- individual Journey issues,
- author opinion.

It may only be amended if:

1. A constitutional contradiction is discovered.
2. A production Journey exposes a constitutional defect that cannot be resolved at the Journey level.
3. New scientific evidence invalidates an existing constitutional law.

Any proposed amendment must pass all five audit gates before it may be ratified.

---

## Disposition of Future Improvements

All future educational improvements, storytelling refinements, and narration corrections shall be implemented within individual Chapters.

Chapter-level improvements do not constitute constitutional changes and do not require a constitutional amendment process.
